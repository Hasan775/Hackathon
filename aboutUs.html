Top Hiking Trails

Shahdag Mountain

Region: Qusar

One of the most iconic hiking destinations in Azerbaijan. Shahdag offers breathtaking mountain views, fresh alpine air, and peaceful nature. The trail ranges from moderate to difficult, making it perfect for hikers seeking adventure and unforgettable landscapes in the Greater Caucasus mountains.

Gobustan National Park

Region: Gobustan

A UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its ancient rock carvings, mud volcanoes, and unique desert-like scenery. This trail is easy and beginner-friendly, suitable for family trips and cultural experiences. A perfect balance between hiking and exploring prehistoric history.

Khinalug Village Trail

Region: Quba

One of the oldest continuously inhabited mountain villages in the world. This trail passes through deep valleys, green slopes, and panoramic ridges. Ideal for those seeking authentic culture, ancient traditions, and dramatic Caucasus views. Moderate difficulty; recommended for all nature lovers.

Hiking Tips

Always carry enough water, wear comfortable hiking shoes, check weather conditions, and respect the local environment. Hiking in Azerbaijan offers a variety of terrains, from mountains to semi-deserts, so plan accordingly.
