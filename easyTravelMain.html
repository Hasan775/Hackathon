Our Project

Our project is dedicated to helping hikers discover free and accessible hiking spots in Azerbaijan. Using our platform, users can find trails, hidden gems, and scenic locations without any entrance fees. We aim to make hiking more affordable, enjoyable, and accessible for everyone, while promoting safety, sustainability, and exploration of the country's natural beauty.

Hiking Tips

Always carry enough water, wear comfortable hiking shoes, check weather conditions, and respect the local environment. Hiking in Azerbaijan offers a variety of terrains, from mountains to semi-deserts, so plan accordingly.
